The Long


Born in Rajasthan, brought up in Nagpur, Kolkatta, Jammu, Bangalore, Hyderabad, he is a personified version of "Unity in diversity". Son of a highly spiritual mother and a hardworking father. He has survived through 12 years of schooling, 4 years of engineering just like every other youngster in India. As each year passed by, his interest in books and writing grew leaps and bounds. Wattpad was a platform he heavily relied upon, when it came to reading and writing when he started his journey. He has a younger brother and another younger brother which implies he has 2 younger brothers which in turn implies he is the eldest brother which in turn implies the responsibilities and sacrifices that come with the title. He isn't particularly athletic and 25 out of 24 hours in his everyday life go into sitting on a chair or sleeping dreaming about how his life would be when he becomes successful, how everyday he will get up from his bed to make a change to the society, how everyday he wouldn't have to introduce himself as he is doing now in this paragraph. He is yet to claim a name for himself in literature. Also he plans not to claim his name in his favorite field:Mechanical Engineering. He has decided to move his career totally to writing in, Oh Not Yet. He is searching for jobs as he types this 3rd person introduction about himself. 1 out of 100 people who start following their passions become successful, he is the captain of the team 99.
