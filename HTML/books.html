Private Investigator



Intro

A bisexual engaged to her late 20s boyfriend.Owner and founder of a failed security company, a company which claims to provide protection from the sorts. Her life is shredded and torn apart when she finds her boyfriend shot down while hunting the international diamond smuggles Houdini. She craves revenge, she breathes revenge, she bleeds revenge. Depression follows through tragic events and came with it an obsession, obsession against the most dangerous smuggler. To complete her bloodlust, she takes off on a solo mission but fate gifts her with a handsome psychiatrist and a beautiful chemist to assist her and complete the hunt of Houdini





Psychiatrist



Intro

This book deals with that part of society which has already gone through so many problems to even be recognised. It deals with their problems and how the society judges them. It deals with their day to day life. It teaches us amidst all the hardships we go through to succeed there is someone else who has gone through tougher times, unbearable scenarios and if they can go through that we can too

Chemist



Intro

An Indian girl who is afraid to come out as a lesbian because she things her family won't approve of her identity. She is the type who doesn't usually go against the society.A girl with an emotional quotient, also one of the brightest minds in the field of chemistry all over nation.She finds inspiration in everything and anything that is rebellious. She spends most of her time in labs trying to fit in the stereotypes of a girl without letting anyone see through her. Follow her story, a story that leads her to be a proud lesbian who stumbles upon Anya and accompanies her in the hunt of Houdini.
