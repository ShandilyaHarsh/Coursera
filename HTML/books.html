Know your characters





Private Investigator



Intro

A bisexual engaged to hir late 20s boyfriend.Owner and founder of a failed security company, a company which claims to provide protection from the sorts. Hir life is shredded and torn apart when ze finds hir boyfriend shot down while hunting the International Diamond smuggler Houdini. Ze craves revenge, Ze breathes revenge, Ze bleeds revenge. Depression follows through the tragic events and came with it an obsession, obsession against the most dangerous smuggler in the world. To complete hir bloodlust, ze takes off on a solo mission but fate gifts hir with a handsome psychologist and a beautiful chemist to assist hir and complete the hunt of Houdini





Psychologist



Intro

A person with the 6th highest recored IQ, Siddharth Kamath is a consultant and psychologist of the highest calibre in Indian police force.The genius living in his mid 20s is yet to learn a lot in terms of emotions and the fact that he possesses a below average EQ makes it all the more difficult.A highly blunt and aggressive man who is trying to find logic in anything and everything. "Sid the shrink" is a man trying to earn respect in his workspace battling against the people who treat his abilities as some sort of a party trick. He is a cis-male who meets Anya on her quest to hunt Houdini.He is trying to understand bisexuality, the other identities and his own feelings for Anya. Follow Siddharth Kamath to look through the eyes of an extremely intelligent cis cis-male on a quest of his own trying to find his place in the society as a person and as a psychologist.

Chemist



Intro

An Indian girl who is afraid to come out as a lesbian because she thinks her family won't approve of her identity. She is the type who doesn't usually go against the society.A girl with a high emotional quotient, also one of the brightest minds in the field of chemistry all over nation.She finds inspiration in everything and anything that is rebellious. She spends most of her time in labs trying to fit in the stereotypes of the society without letting anyone see through her. Follow her story, a story that leads her to be a proud lesbian who stumbles upon Anya and accompanies her in the hunt of Houdini.
